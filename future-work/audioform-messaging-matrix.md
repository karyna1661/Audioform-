## 7) Objection Handling Snippets

Objection: "Voice reduces response volume."
- Response: "Yes, and that is often good. We optimize for signal density, not raw volume."

Objection: "Forms already work."
- Response: "Forms capture typed answers. Audioform captures conviction and emotion needed for higher-confidence decisions."

Objection: "This seems like a nice-to-have."
- Response: "For weekly shipping teams, decision clarity is leverage. Better signal compounds each sprint."

## 10) Data-Backed Messaging Rules (Apify Analysis)

Source:
- `future-work/audioform-apify-dataset-insight-report.md`

Rules:
1. Prefer declarative tension statements over generic question-only posts.
2. Use list-structured posts for save/share lift.
3. Keep CTAs short and specific ("Send a 30-second voice take").
4. Use product-loop metrics (TTFR, depth rate, replay rate) instead of broad generic survey stats.
5. Avoid generic "survey tool" language in top-of-funnel posts.
