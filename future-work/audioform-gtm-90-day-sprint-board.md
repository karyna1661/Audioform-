## Sprint 1 (Weeks 1-2): Narrative Foundation + Conversion Infrastructure

Objective:
Finalize category narrative and ensure product surfaces support creator conversion.

Deliverables:
1. Messaging matrix v1 published.
2. Landing page hero/value-prop copy aligned to category claim.
3. Responder -> creator CTA live on thank-you page with tracking.
4. Weekly content brief template created.
5. Channel attribution event plan finalized.

KPIs:
1. Thank-you page creator CTA CTR >= 8%.
2. New creator starts from thank-you path tracked.
3. 100% of outbound content uses approved narrative angles.

Exit criteria:
1. Narrative QA approved by GTM owner.
2. Product + marketing language consistent on top 3 pages.
3. Tracking events validated in event store.

## Sprint 2 (Weeks 3-4): Cornerstone Distribution

Objective:
Establish consistent publishing rhythm and category repetition.

Deliverables:
1. 6 X cornerstone posts.
2. 6 Farcaster cornerstone posts.
3. 1 long-form anchor piece ("Why Public Builders Need High-Signal Feedback Infrastructure").
4. 2 derivative thread packs (8-12 posts each).

KPIs:
1. Qualified profile visits from X/Farcaster.
2. % posts with >2% engagement rate.
3. CTA clicks to signup/survey creation.

Exit criteria:
1. At least 12 primary distribution assets shipped.
2. Top-performing angle identified (conviction/filtering/emotion).

## Sprint 3 (Weeks 5-6): Proof Loop Content

Objective:
Demonstrate workflow utility with product-backed case studies.

Deliverables:
1. 2 public build case studies (ship -> voice feedback -> decision made).
2. "Signal Log" weekly series launched.
3. 1 reusable case study template.

KPIs:
1. Case-study-to-signup conversion rate.
2. Activation rate from case-study traffic.
3. # of users running first voice survey within 24h of signup.

Exit criteria:
1. Both case studies include explicit decision change evidence.
2. Weekly Signal Log cadence maintained.

## Sprint 4 (Weeks 7-8): Searchable Content Layer

Objective:
Build discoverable demand around buyer-intent topics.

Deliverables:
1. 4 SEO posts:
- "How to collect high-signal product feedback"
- "Voice survey vs text survey for startups"
- "Build in public feedback loop template"
- "How to prioritize product feedback by conviction"
2. Internal linking between SEO posts and signup path.
3. Conversion-focused CTA blocks added to each post.

KPIs:
1. Organic impressions/clicks baseline.
2. Time on page and scroll depth.
3. Organic-to-signup conversion.

Exit criteria:
1. 4 posts indexed.
2. Post-to-product CTA tracking verified.

## Sprint 5 (Weeks 9-10): Creator Flywheel

Objective:
Turn individual users into repeat contributors and advocates.

Deliverables:
1. Weekly creator ritual assets ("Ship update + collect 5 voice responses").
2. Build-in-public creator partner list (10 targets).
3. Partner co-marketing outreach kit.

KPIs:
1. # active creators running weekly loops.
2. Creator revisit rate.
3. # partner creators publishing Audioform workflow.

Exit criteria:
1. At least 5 partner conversations initiated.
2. At least 2 partner-created public examples.

## Sprint 6 (Weeks 11-12): Scale Readiness

Objective:
Systematize GTM for broader beta expansion.

Deliverables:
1. Repeatable campaign template library.
2. Monthly reporting dashboard with KPI tree.
3. Beta expansion launch package.
4. Voice feedback benchmark draft report.

KPIs:
1. CAC-to-activation trend.
2. Activation-to-retention trend.
3. Content production velocity by channel.

Exit criteria:
1. Weekly GTM cycle runs without ad hoc coordination.
2. Launch package approved for wider beta rollout.

## Weekly Ritual Template

Monday:
1. Select one tension statement.
2. Select one primary angle.
3. Publish weekly brief from `npm run gtm:brief`.

Wednesday:
1. Asset production checkpoint.
2. Channel preflight QA.

Friday:
1. KPI review and top learnings.
2. Keep/kill/iterate decisions.
3. Publish dogfooding proof loop artifact ("what we heard -> what we changed").

## Owner Model (Initial)

1. Head of GTM + PMM: strategy, messaging, launch governance.
2. Content + Distribution Lead: asset production and channel execution.
3. Growth Ops (fractional): measurement, attribution, reporting.
