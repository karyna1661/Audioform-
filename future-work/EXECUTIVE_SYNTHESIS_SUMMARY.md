## Core Moat: Question Intelligence

### Three Golden Question Types

#### 1. Moment Questions (Activate Memory)
❌ Bad: "What do you think about onboarding?"  
✅ Good: "Can you describe the exact moment during onboarding when you felt confused or almost quit?"

**Why:** Triggers episodic memory → produces stories, not opinions

---

#### 2. Almost Quit Questions (Activate Friction)
❌ Bad: "Did you encounter challenges?"  
✅ Good: "At what moment did you almost stop using the product? What almost made you give up?"

**Why:** Humans remember negative experiences more vividly

---

#### 3. Magic Wand Questions (Activate Imagination)
❌ Bad: "Any suggestions?"  
✅ Good: "If you could magically change one thing about this product, what would it be and why?"

**Why:** Removes constraints, reveals highest priorities

---

### Questions That Kill Voice Responses

**AVOID:**
- ❌ Rating questions ("Rate 1-10") → "Seven." (2 seconds)
- ❌ Yes/No questions → "Yes." (1 second)
- ❌ Generic opinions ("What do you think?") → Vague rambling
